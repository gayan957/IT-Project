1. **Google UK/US English Female** (Premium quality)
2. **Microsoft Zira/Hazel** (Windows systems)
3. **Samantha/Victoria/Allison/Ava** (Apple systems)
4. **Chrome Female voices** (Browser-optimized)

### Text Processing Pipeline
```
Raw ChatBot Response 
    ↓
Remove Markdown Formatting (**bold**, *italic*, `code`)
    ↓
Strip Emojis and Special Characters (Unicode cleanup)
    ↓
Convert Bullet Points to Natural Speech
    ↓
Add Natural Pauses and Emphasis
    ↓
Enhanced Natural Speech Output
```

### Speech Parameter Optimization
- **Standard Voice**: Rate 1.0, Pitch 1.0, Volume 1.0 (robotic)
- **Enhanced Voice**: Rate 0.75, Pitch 1.2, Volume 0.8 (natural female)
